Studio Menu

Face-Up Service

Professional face-up using high-grade materials. Sealed with MSC UV Cut. Standard price includes glitter, small tattoos, birthmarks and freckles. A digital mockup will be provided before work commences. Changes can be made during the process as progress will be shared. You will provide your own head.

Wait Time: 8-10 Weeks

SmartDoll Face-Up Bundle

Everything included in the Faceup service but we will provide a Chaos grade head. These heads may have minor flaws and imperfections. No headback is included. Please contact us for currently available skintones and sculpts.

Wait Time: 8-10 Weeks

Dollfie Dream Face-Up Bundle

Everything included in the Faceup service but we will provide a Dollfie Dream/Mini Dollfie Dream head. Headback is included. Please contact us for currently available skintones and sculpts.

Wait Time: 8-10 Weeks

Manicure/Pedicure

A simple paintjob on the dolls existing finger/toe nails. Blushing is optional, please let us know your preference at the time of comissioning. You provide the hands/feet.

Wait: 1-2 Weeks

Acrylic Manicure/Pedicure

Fully built acrylic nails for fingers or toes. Designs and shape can be discussed. You can provide your own hands or enquire about current stock. We typically don't carry stock of feet. Service is possible for all dolls. We can only provide parts for SmartDoll and Dollfie Dream at this time.

Wait: 2-3 Weeks

Decora Hands

Decora hands come fully decorated on both nails and hands - Accessories are random but you can choose colours and theme. Larger accessories can be chosen if desired. Designs and shape can be discussed. You can provide your own hands or enquire about current stock. We typically don't carry stock of feet. Service is possible for all dolls. We can only provide parts for SmartDoll and Dollfie Dream at this time.

Wait: 2-3 Weeks

Follow-Me Eyes

Handcrafted glass eyes designed to follow you from every angle. All designs are created and assembled by us in the studio.

Wait: Up to 1 week

Glitter Follow-Me Eyes

Our classic follow-me eyes with a glittery top coat over resin on the Iris. All designs are created and assembled by us in the studio.

Wait: Up to 2 weeks

Custom Follow-Me Eyes

Need a specific character's eyes? Have your own design? We can recreate them in doll scale follow-me eyes! All designs are created and assembled by us in the studio.

Wait: Up to 2 weeks
