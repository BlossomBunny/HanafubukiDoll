HanafubukiDoll

Hi! We are Dani and Jas, the people behind HanafubukiDoll.

Our story started with a Boy&Girl Natalia and an Angel of Dream Chi all the way back in 2008. Dolls have come and gone over the years, friends all over the world have been made and a passion for creativity has been reignited.

These days our mission is to create a safe and positive experience for doll collectors where they can see their dreams come to life.
